What is this? Select the home NHL Team of your choosing then enter your name and click 'pick player' to randomly generate players from that team. Repeat this with your friend(s) names.

Then select the away team and repeat, allowing you to have players from both teams.

Watch the game and if your player scores or lets in a goal? Grab a beer or shot (or get creative with the rules). Enjoy!
